## Cookie Policy

### What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites.

### Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Essential | Site functionality, shopping cart | Session |
| Analytics | Google Analytics (anonymized) | 2 years |
| Preferences | Remember your settings | 1 year |

### Managing Cookies
You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some site features may not work properly without cookies.
