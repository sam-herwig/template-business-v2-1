## Our Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we'll make it right.

---

## 14-Day Refund Window

You may request a full refund within **14 days** of purchase if:

✅ You haven't downloaded the template files yet
✅ The template has a significant technical defect that we cannot resolve
✅ The template was misrepresented in our marketing materials

---

## Refunds NOT Provided For

We cannot offer refunds for:

❌ Change of mind after downloading the files
❌ Lack of technical knowledge to implement the template
❌ Incompatibility with hosting/tools not listed in requirements
❌ Purchasing the wrong template (please review demos before buying)
❌ Issues caused by your own modifications
❌ Requests made after the 14-day window

---

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email **support@masssoftware.dev** with:
   - Your order number
   - Reason for refund request
   - Any relevant screenshots or details

2. We'll respond within **2 business days**

3. Approved refunds are processed within **5-7 business days** to your original payment method

## Bundle Purchases

If you purchased a bundle and want a refund:
- If no templates downloaded: Full refund available
- If some templates downloaded: Partial refund may be offered at our discretion
